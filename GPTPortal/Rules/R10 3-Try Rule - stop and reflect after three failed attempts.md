# ✅ 3-Try Rule: stop and reflect after three failed attempts

> If we fail to achieve our goal within three tries, stop, step back, and reflect on precisely what information you need from me.

**Type:** PRESCRIPTION  ·  **Hand:** —  ·  **LLM:** PRESCRIPTION

## Judgment
A bounded STOP-and-ask rule — the rare concrete trigger (three tries) in an otherwise self-assessed file.
